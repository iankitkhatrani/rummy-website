RummyLegit TDS Policy

Understanding the New Tax Rules:

As per the Income Tax Act 1961, RummyLegit adheres to new regulations mandating a 30% tax deduction on users' Net Winnings during withdrawal or on 31st March (end of the Financial Year). Any Income from online games from the said date shall be taxable under Section 115BBJ ( Charging Section) Under the Income Tax Act 1961.
As per the Income Tax Act 1961, RummyLegit adheres to new regulations mandates any person paying an Income by way of winnings from any online game during the financial year shall deduct 30% Tax on the net winnings in his/her user account under Section 194BA of Income Tax Act 1961.

Defining 'Net Winnings':

Net Winnings are the difference between total withdrawals and total deposits within a financial year. For example:
- Total Deposit: ₹2000
- Total Withdrawal: ₹3000
- Net Winnings = ₹3000 - ₹2000 = ₹1000

Tax Calculation on 'Net Winnings':

Tax is assessed at 30% of cumulative Net Winnings during withdrawal. For instance:
- Net Winnings: ₹1000
- Tax = 30% of ₹1000 = ₹300

Deposits

Total Deposit= Deposits in the Financial Year + Opening Balance of the Financial Year.
-Any net winnings which has suffered TDS at the time of any earlier withdrawals would also be reduced while computing net winning at the time of withdrawal.

Deduction Timing:

Tax is deducted when a player withdraws or on 31st March (end of the Financial Year). The total tax liability on net winnings for the year is deducted from the player's wallet.

Exceptions:

- No tax if total withdrawal is less than total deposit.
- Negative Net Winnings result in no tax deduction.

Account Balance Consideration:

- Opening A/c Balance at Financial Year start is considered in Net Winnings calculation.
- If Net Winnings are negative, no tax is deducted.
- Any remaining amount at year-end is considered a withdrawal and taxed.

Refunds and Tax Certificates:

- Players can claim refunds by filing returns with the income tax department.
- Tax certificates will be issued within 1 month from the last date of filing TDS returns for every quarter.

Common Wallet Across Games:

- Players with common wallets across Games24x7 games (e.g., RummyCircle & My11Circle) have entry fees deducted from overall winnings for taxation at a 30% rate.

Calculation Method Change:

- Previous method: (Total Winnings) - (Total Entry Fees)
- Revised method (effective April 1, 2023): (Total Withdrawals) - (Total Deposits)
Note: RummyLegit reserves the right to modify the TDS policy in accordance with any future regulatory changes. Any alterations will be communicated to users in advance.

FAIR PLAY POLICY

At Legitplay , we are committed to providing a fair and secure gaming environment for all players. Our Fair Play Policy is designed to ensure that our games are played in a transparent, secure, and responsible manner.

Transparency:

We clearly explain the rules and game mechanics of Rummy Legit, and our algorithms are regularly audited to ensure fairness and randomness.

Privacy:

it is strictly prohibited for a user to use his/her user ID to disclose personal information like email address, web address, phone number or any such details.

Obscenity/ Abuse:

We do not permit vulgar, Obscene or abusive chats in our chat windows.

No Collusion:

We have implemented measures to prevent players from colluding or sharing information during the game.

Responsible Gaming:

We encourage players to play responsibly and within their means, with options for self- exclusion and limits on playing time and stakes.

Dispute Resolution:

We have a clear process for resolving disputes and addressing players concerns.

Secure Payments:

We ensure secure and trusted payment processing, with clear policies for deposits, withdrawals, and refunds.

Player Protection:

We implement measures to protect player data and ensure a safe and secure gaming environment.

Regular Audits:

We conduct regular audits to ensure compliance with our Fair Play Policy and maintain the integrity of the game.

Players Support:

We provide easily accessible support for players, including FAQs, contact forms and customer support teams.
By playing on our platform, you agree to abide by our Fair Play Policy and contribute to a fair play and enjoyable gaming experience for all players .

RummyLegit Refund Policy

Cancellation and Refund in Rummy Cash Games:

In the rare occurrence of a Rummy Cash Game cancellation, whether due to technical errors solely attributable to the Platform or Fair Play Violations, the following settlement policy shall be implemented:

Points Rummy Cash Games:

In the event of Points Rummy Cash Game cancellation at any stage:
- The add-cash amount paid by all Genuinely Affected Users will be promptly refunded.
- For Users involved in Fair Play Violation, the add-cash amount will be forfeited, and a Fair Play violation fee may be imposed as per this Policy.

Pool Rummy, Deals Rummy Cash Games, and Tournaments:

In case of cancellation of Pool Rummy Cash Games, Deals Rummy Cash Games, or Tournament Games at any stage:
- The entry fee paid by all Genuinely Affected Users, including rebuy amounts if any, will be refunded.
- For Users engaging in Fair Play Violation, the entry fee and rebuy amounts, if any, will be forfeited.
Additionally, a Fair Play violation fee may be imposed as per this Policy.

Void Tournaments:

Tournament will be cancelled and any entry tickets used by players will be refunded.

Refund Processing:

Refunds, following a cancellation and subsequent review by the RummyLegit team in accordance with this Policy, will be initiated within 14 (fourteen) days of receiving the refund request.

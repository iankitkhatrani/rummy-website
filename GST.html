RummyLegit GST Policy on Cash Deposits

Effective Date: 1st October 2023

At RummyLegit, we strive to ensure transparency and compliance with all government regulations regarding online gaming. In accordance with the latest government mandate, a Goods and Services Tax (GST) of 28% is applicable on each add cash transaction towards online money gaming. This tax is levied by the government, and RummyLegit has no role in the determination or alteration of this tax rate.

GST Calculation and Wallet Credits:

When you add cash to your RummyLegit account, the 28% GST will be calculated as part of your deposited amount. Here’s how it works using a ₹500 deposit as an example:

- Total Deposit: ₹500 (This is the amount you choose to add to your RummyLegit account).
- GST Calculation (28%):The effective contribution towards gameplay, after excluding GST, is calculated by dividing the deposit by 1.28. For a ₹500 deposit, this calculation is ₹500 / 1.28 = ₹390.625.
- GST Amount: The GST amount is the difference between your total deposit and the effective contribution towards gameplay. From the ₹500 deposit, the GST would be ₹500 - ₹390.625 = ₹109.375.
To ensure clarity and fairness, the GST amount will be reflected in your bonus wallet, while the remaining amount, in this example, ₹390.625, will be credited to your cash wallet.
- Bonus Wallet (GST Amount): ₹109.375 - Cash Wallet: ₹390.625

Total Wallet Reflection:

Despite the division between your cash wallet and bonus wallet, your main account balance will still reflect the total amount deposited, ₹500, inclusive of GST. This approach ensures that the total value of your deposit remains accessible for gameplay, in line with our commitment to provide a transparent and user-friendly gaming experience.

Policy Rationale:

This policy is designed to comply with government regulations while minimising the impact on your gaming experience. We understand the importance of clear communication regarding any charges applied to your transactions and are committed to providing our users with detailed information on how their funds are managed.
Note: Please be aware that this policy is subject to change in response to any future government directives or tax rate adjustments. RummyLegit will keep all players informed of any such changes in a timely manner.
